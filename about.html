About Londa Education Society
Before 60 years Londa and the villages around the 50 Km radius of Londa have no any facility of High School Education So some visionaries in the Londa got together to solve this problem and We have founded Londa Education Society in 1962 in Londa which is the remote place in khanapur Taluka. Since than through the work in the educational field we have creatively contributed not only in the development of the remote places around Londa but also for the National Development. We have completed the 60 years of our valuable and inspirational work and also to work firmly in this field recently in this year we have started P.U. College.
Since last 59 year we are running Londa High School, Londa with Marathi and Kannada Medium and since 39 years we are running English medium school from LKG to Standard 10th and since one year Londa Composite Pre-University College.
Our extreme efforts are to facilitate the students residing at remote places and dense forest areas in and around of 50 km radius there by helping the noble cause of education.
When there was no any facility of education in and around the area. We started the school only with noble intention of educating the mass without charging any school fees, which proved fruitful and we have achieved educational progress of the 52 villages around the Londa.
Thus we have spread the light of knowledge, culture and new hope of life in the hut and hamlets of poor and innocent people.
Our Beliefs and Objectives
Beliefs
  • Students First
  • Knowledge Temple
  • Effiicient Teachers
  • Holistic Development
  • Respect Everyone
  • Continuous Improvement
  • System of Support
  • Partnership with Parents
Objectives
  • Providing a child a friendly, healthy and safe atmosphere that will empower every child to overcome their limitations.
  • Provide a foundation and ensure that concepts are so well understood that they can intelligently apply them to other situations.
  • Learning is possible only in the mirror of relatonship and not in isolation.
  • Teach to the agreed upon course objectives and provide evidence of student achievement of those objectives.
  • To work upon maximising student's potential through self development programs like yoga sports recreation and culture travel.
  • Inculcate a deep rooted respect in students for environment , people and resources.
  • We focus on respect and self-responsibility. The management works upon providing opportunities for student's voice to influence school decisions.
  • Provide professional development and technologies necessary to deliver the curriculum and to access and evaluate student related information.
